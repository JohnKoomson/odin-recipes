Ghana Gari

Ghanaian gari is a very serious meal,very affordable to provide or prepare.

Ingredients

  • Gari
  • Groundnut
  • Sugart
  • Water
  • Milk

Steps

  1. In a large bowl or cup, pour in gari. You can add more quantity when needed
  2. Add little water to gari yo make a paste. Add the 3 spoons of milk to form paste and mix well
  3. Add the sugar to mixture and combine completely.
  4. Fill the cup with water to make mixture lighter. Then add your groundnut.
